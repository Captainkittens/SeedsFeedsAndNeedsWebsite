Seeds Feeds and Needs

This is what we're doing..

A startup starting from Edmonton, Alberta, a next generation cooperative.

In business chatter, it would be building a vertically, and horizontally, integrated sets of parallel systems intended to produce social justice and produce.

What we're trying to really do is have some fun, build a great community, and try our best to make a positive difference.

We're selecting agriculture as one of our first large scale projects to tackle and have so far made undoubtable progress.

We have over a hundred active members looking to discover a new way to become more self reliant and grow.

What we've accomplished so far..

Originating as a group of strong-willed individuals who collected themselves together into an anachronistic self-supportive community, this cooperative began in 2012 as the Occupy Camp in Edmonton.

After that camp got, erhmm, crushed, by EPS. (right or wrong?, at this point.. w/e..)

So some shout outs were made, we walked the high level bridge, and more. 'Listen up! There is a huge assortment of issues plaguing us today! Imagine the exponential growth quotient if only we could dedicate a small bit of your time in order to work together! Please consider that ever sway away is setting unwelcome precident. Horay/Yipee/Woohoo for education!.'

So things obviously had to take a new direction, and so started the beginning Seeds Feeds and Needs, the cooperative.

Basic foundations of the cooperative were established when we began as resellers of high quality organic produce.

With only the greatest thanks to the folks who allowed us to its use, a small test field for direct procurement was established.

Many lessons were learned and in the end, a yield was harvested, and was distribruted, and now the next season is starting.
